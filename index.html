Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I register to vote?

You can register online through our website or visit your local election office for assistance.

2. What do I need to register?

Typically, you will need a valid ID and proof of residence. Check your local requirements for specifics.

3. When is the deadline to register?

Registration deadlines vary by state. Please check the voter registration page for your state’s deadline.

4. How do I know if I'm registered?

You can check your registration status on our website or contact your local election office.

5. What should I do if I encounter problems on election day?

If you experience any issues, contact the election hotline for immediate assistance.
